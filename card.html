Peafowl

Predominately blue with feathers that rise into a fan during courtship, the Peafowl, or most notably, the Peacock , symbolizes luxury, beauty, pride and protection.

  • Scientific Name: Peafowl
  • Wingspan: 35-43 Inches
  • Lifespan: 15 Years
  • Habitat:Forests and Trees

The Peafowl's feathers are covered in tiny crystal-like structures and only the males are called by the name Peacock.

Peahen are also a part of the Peafowl family and are female.
They are considered excellent and instinctively driven mothers.

The bird is considered divine and is said to be a mystic guardian.
